💰 Economy

  • /balance — Check your coins
  • /beg — Beg for coins
  • /daily — Claim daily coins
  • /work — Earn coins
  • /transfer — Transfer coins to another player

🃏 Card Collecting

  • /collection — View your card collection
  • /completion — Check your completion percentage
  • /info — Get info about a specific card
  • /gift — Gift a card to another player
  • /wish — Make a wish for a card
  • /last — View the last spawned card

⚙ Utilities

  • /setspawnchannel — Set the spawn channel
  • /invite — Get the bot invite link

🏆 Leaderboard & Profile

  • /top — See the leaderboard
  • /profile — View your player profile
